About This Event

Build for Tomorrow is a 48-hour hackathon where developers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to build innovative solutions that address real-world challenges. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or a first-time participant, this event has something for everyone.

What to expect:

  • 48 hours of non-stop building and collaboration
  • Mentorship from industry experts and successful founders
  • Workshops on AI/ML, Web3, and FinTech
  • Free meals, snacks, and beverages throughout the event
  • $10,000 in prizes across 5 categories

Teams of 2-5 members can participate. Solo participants will be matched with teams during the event.
