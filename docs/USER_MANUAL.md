# StartupEvents — Platform User Manual 🚀

Welcome to the official manual for StartupEvents. This guide covers all functionalities for our three main user roles: **Attendee**, **Organizer**, and **Platform Admin**.

---

## 👥 1. For Attendees (Community Members)

As an Attendee, you are here to discover and participate in the startup ecosystem.

### **Browsing & Discovery**

1. **Explore Events**: Visit the **Events** page to see all upcoming meetups, hackathons, and workshops.
2. **Search & Filter**: Use the top search bar to find specific titles or filter by category (e.g., *Hackathon, Workshop, Pitch Night*).
3. **AI Recommendations**: Found on your **Dashboard**, our AI suggests events based on your profile and interests.

### **Participating (RSVP)**

1. **View Details**: Click on any event card to see full descriptions, date, city, and attendee count.
2. **Registration**: Click the **🚀 RSVP Now** button on any event page.
3. **Manage RSVPs**: Go to your **Dashboard > My RSVPs** to see a list of all events you are registered for.

### **Growing into a Host**

1. **Host an Event**: In your dashboard sidebar, click **🚀 Host an Event**.
2. **Confirmation**: Read the Role Transition Warning (Note: You lose RSVP powers once verified).
3. **Application**: Click **Apply Anyway**. Your status will change to `Pending`.
4. **Revocation**: If you change your mind while pending, click **Revoke Request** in your dashboard to stay as an attendee.

---

## 🛠️ 2. For Organizers (Event Hosts)

As an Organizer, you are the driver of community content.

### **Event Management**

1. **Dashboard Overview**: View your total RSVPs, active events, and recent activity on your main dashboard.
2. **Create an Event**:
   * Click **Create Event** in the sidebar.
   * Fill in Title, Date, Category, Location, and Description.
   * **Note**: If you are not yet "Verified" by an admin, your event will save as `Pending Approval` and wait for moderation.
3. **Manage Existing Events**: Use the **Manage Events** tab to delete or modify your hosted listings.

### **Verification Process**

1. **Awaiting Approval**: If you see a blue banner titled **🛡️ Verification Pending**, you can create events but cannot make them "Live" yet.
2. **Full Access**: Once verified by an admin, your "Publish" button unlocks, and your events go live immediately upon creation.

### **Analytics**

1. **Performance Check**: Visit the **📈 Analytics** tab to see deep metrics on event growth and attendee demographics across your portfolio.

---

## 🛡️ 3. For Platform Admins (System Operators)

Admins have full oversight and moderation control over the ecosystem.

### **User Moderation**

1. **Community Directory**: Access the **👥 Users** tab in your dashboard.
2. **Role Management**: Use the dropdown next to any user to change their role between *Attendee, Organizer,* or *Admin*.
3. **Account Suspension**: Click the **🚫 Suspend** button to temporarily disable any user's access.

### **Organizer Verification (The Gatekeeper)**

1. **Approve Applications**: In the **Users** tab, look for users with a **🕒 Pending** status in the Verification column.
2. **Approve/Reject**:
   * Click **Approve** to officially promote them to Organizer.
   * Click **Reject** to send them back to Attendee status. (Note: This triggers a 20-day cooldown before they can re-apply).

### **Platform Oversight**

1. **Systems Intelligence**: Your dashboard is the **Platform Intelligence Center**. You see platform-wide stats (total users, total events across all organizers).
2. **Event Oversight**: You can view and manage *every* event on the platform, regardless of who organized it.

---

## ⚙️ Account Settings (All Roles)

1. **Profile Update**: Go to **Settings** in the sidebar to update your name, bio, or interests.
2. **Identity Sync**: Any changes made here reflect instantly across your event cards and organizers' attendee lists.
3. **Logout**: Use the **🚪 Logout** button at the bottom of the sidebar to securely exit your session.
